Nature

Ukraine is a country of different landscapes: from mountains to the sea, from mountain valleys to steppes. No wonder there are so many amazing and different parks. We have something to be proud of and a place to enjoy nature. Of course, Ukraine is picturesque. Many places of our country are attracted by the mysterious nature and can safely ompete with the landscapes of other countries.

Askania-Nova Biosphere Reserve

This reserve on the territory of Ukraine is unique. For every visitor Askania-Nova offers the opportunity to see not only the arboretum and zoo, but also the virgin steppe. He retained his original inviolability. Here you can see the animals in their natural guise.

Where: Kherson region, Chaplinsky district, Askania-Nova township.

Synevyr National Nature Park

Synevyr is a park of incredible beauty. There is everything: mountains, forest, lake and swamps. Once upon a time, this park was a seabed, and now everyone can walk through it, breathe fresh air and reunite with nature. The only "Forest and Rafting Museum" in Europe is located in the park.

Where: Transcarpathian region, Mizhhirya district.

Shatsky National Nature Park

Shatsky National Nature Park is exactly the place where you can relax not only body but also soul. Incredible landscapes will amaze even those people who do not love nature. The eco-system of lake-swamp-forest complexes in this park is considered to be the largest in Europe.

Where: in the Volyn region.
